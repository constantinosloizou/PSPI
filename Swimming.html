    Swimming at Molos Limassol

    At 15 of June at 8 o' clock in the morning we are swimming all together in Limassol. The purpose of the event is to raise awareness of the need to protect our marine ecosystems. The swimmers will be given masks in order to see the fishes that inhabit our seas.
    After the swimming we will be given a briefing about the importance of the marine ecosystems by a professional. Join us to enjoy our swim together!!

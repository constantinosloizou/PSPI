    Personal information is information that identifies a specific individual. We collect this information for registered users of the Services and for visitors who use the Site to learn more about us. You may visit the Site without setting up an account, but the available functionality to you will be limited. When you engage in certain activities that we make available on the Site, such as opening an account, submitting or posting content we may ask you to provide certain personal information.

    • Account Registration and Profiles:

    Your name and email address or via single sign-on with Google, Facebook or Clever login information is required to create an account. Our account profile allows you to provide additional optional biographical information to us such as social media and website addresses. In addition, you can upload or change your profile picture and you will have the opportunity to upload files to your profile.

    If you contact us to request additional information or to submit comments we will collect the information you submitted. We will use this information to respond to your request or to inform you about program services and events. Email messages we send in this regard will permit you to unsubscribe from future such messages.

    When you interact with us through various social media - for example, by liking us on Facebook - we may collect information including your email address, location and other biographical information. The information we receive from your social network depends on your privacy settings. You should always review and, if you prefer, adjust your privacy settings on third-party websites and services before linking or connecting them to our Services.

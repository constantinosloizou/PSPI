    Trees Planting at Troodos Mountain

    Our Action

    At 6 of June we are planting trees at Troodos Mountain in order to provide another green landscape. Because of the last year's fire an amount of the pines have been burned so we are going to plant 40 new pines beacause we have to stop deforestation.
    We are taking all of the safety measures for the volunteers who would like to help us. Everyone will be given boots, protective gloves and glasses to prevent any injury.

    If you wonder why is it so important to plant trees remember: Trees help clean the air we breathe, filter the water we drink, and provide habitat to over 80% of the world's terrestrial biodiversity. Forests provide jobs to over 1.6 billion people, absorb harmful carbon from the atmosphere, and are key ingredients in 25% of all medicines. Did you ever taken an Aspirin? It comes from the bark of a tree! Join us to make our goal real!!

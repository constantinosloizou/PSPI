    Beach Cleanup at Ladys Mile Beach in Limassol

    Our Action

    At 25 of May at 8 o' clock in the mornig we are organizing a beach cleanup at Ladys Mile in Limassol. There is a lot of trash and we are going to collect as many items as possible to make the beach clean again.
    A list of the main items we are going to collect in trash bags:

  • Plastic bags
  • Bottles and cans
  • Fishing gear
  • Cigarettes

    • Any more dangerous items like batteries, electronics, broken glass and others will pe placed in containers with screw on lids for our protection.
    All of the volunteers will be splitted in 2 teams. The first team will collect the rubbish and the second will sort recycling from the trash. For protection the volunteers will be given protective gloves and it would be good to wear closed-toe shoes that are waterpoof. You can also bring your own protective gloves. We recommend you to wear hats and sunglasses in order to protect yourself from the sun.

    The benefit from cleaning the beaching will be big. Apart from people who will hang around, a lot of marine animals also depend on the beach ecosystem.

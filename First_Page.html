    Protect the ecosystems

    The Earth is a fine place and worth fighting for

    TAKE ACTION NOW!

    Due to the pressures of overconsumption, population growth and technology, the biophysical environment is being degraded. Μan destroys the environment every day and if we do nothing, humanity will be destroyed together. The only solution to this problem is to protect the environment. Environmental protection means ecosystem protection. But what is an ecosystem? An ecosystem is a community of living organisms in conjunction with the nonliving components of their environment, interacting as a system. Ecosystems are the foundations of the Biosphere and they determine the health of the entire earth system. In an ecosystem, each organism has its own niche or role to play.
